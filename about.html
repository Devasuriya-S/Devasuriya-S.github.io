Professional Profile

Developing the judgement behind the design.

I am a final-year Master of Professional Engineering student based in Perth, developing my career in industrial electrical design, power systems and renewable energy.

My Direction

From engineering fundamentals to practical project work.

My objective is to develop the technical foundation and engineering judgement expected of a graduate electrical engineer.

I am particularly interested in industrial power distribution, electrical system design, power-system studies, equipment selection, cable engineering and renewable-energy integration.

Through academic work, practical industry exposure and self-directed engineering projects, I am learning how electrical designs progress from initial requirements and concept development through detailed design, procurement, construction and commissioning.

I focus on understanding why each engineering document, calculation and design decision is required—not only how to produce the final deliverable.
